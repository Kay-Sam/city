WHY CITY OF IDEAS?

Your Ideas Deserve to Be Heard

Every day, brilliant people stay silent because they fear speaking, presenting, or being judged. We help you replace fear with confidence so your voice can create opportunities, influence decisions, and inspire change.

Speak Confidently

Engage Any Audience

Our Mission

To raise a generation of confident communicators who can present ideas, lead conversations, and create meaningful impact in their workplaces, businesses, communities, and beyond.

Our Vision

To become a global ecosystem where ideas are transformed into influence, and individuals are empowered to lead with clarity, courage, and purpose.

Meet the Convener

Glory Agunbiade

Founder, City of Ideas

I help leaders, visionaries, entrepreneurs, and changemakers transform their ideas into influence that moves people, opens doors, and drives growth.

For years, I watched brilliant people struggle to communicate their vision. They had great ideas, valuable expertise, and the potential to create impact, yet many remained unheard because they lacked the confidence and communication skills to express themselves effectively.

That reality inspired the birth of City of Ideas. Today, as a public speaker, executive coach, economist, and community builder, I help individuals develop the confidence, clarity, and leadership presence required to thrive in every room they enter.

My mission is simple: to help people find their voice, communicate with purpose, and lead with influence.
