About Glory Agunbiade

Public speaker, executive coach, leadership trainer, event host, songwriter, and community builder.

She helps people find their voice and lead with influence.

Glory founded City of Ideas to help leaders, visionaries, entrepreneurs, women, and changemakers communicate clearly, lead courageously, and turn their ideas into visible impact.

Her work blends practical communication frameworks, executive coaching, stage training, creative expression, community building, and faith-rooted empowerment.

Through City of Ideas and The Awesome Power In Women Ministries Int'l, she creates rooms where people are equipped to speak, heal, grow, and lead.
