Equipping women for spiritual strength and visible impact.

The ministry creates safe and powerful spaces for prayer, teaching, mentorship, leadership development, and honest conversations.

Mission and Vision

The Awesome Power In Women Ministries Int'l exists to raise spiritually grounded, healed, bold, and impactful women.

Mission

To empower women through faith, mentorship, prayer, healing conversations, and leadership development so they can live with identity, confidence, wisdom, and purpose.

Vision

To become a global women empowerment movement where women are spiritually strengthened, emotionally restored, and released to lead in ministry, family, business, career, and society.
